My favorite sport

Sport plays an important part in the life of our state. It is very popular in Ukraine. Football, volleyball and basketball are the main sports.

My favorite sport is the football. When I was like a little boy, I liked it. I like watching football on the TV or visiting some stadium to it.

In the summer 2012 the Europe Football Cup was held in Ukraine. My schoolmate Igor and me travelled to the city Kyiv to see football match "Ukraine-Sweden". It was nice travelling for us, fortunately our team managed to win this game. The hero of the game was A.Shevchenko. I always will remember that day.
